Privacy Policy - BEC Studo

Effective Date: January 07, 2023

At BECStudo, accessible from the Google Play Store, protecting the privacy of our users is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information. By using our app, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

If you have any questions, please contact us at itkonnects@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and enhance your experience with our app:

1. Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and Registration number when creating an account.
  • If you contact us directly, additional information such as the content of messages, attachments, or other details you provide may also be collected.

2. Non-Personal Information

  • App Usage Data: App activity, error reports, and usage trends to improve performance.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

3. Login Information

We only collect your login email to enhance your privacy and user experience. No other login or browser-related data is stored.

How We Use Your Information

  • Operating and maintaining the app.
  • Enhancing and personalizing your app experience.
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer support.
  • Sending updates, promotional materials, or notifications (if consented to).
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, such as Google AdMob, to display advertisements. These services may collect and use data per their privacy policies. For more details, review Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not share or sell your data to any other third parties.

Data Protection Rights

Under applicable laws such as GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Restriction: Request limited processing of your data.
  • Portability: Request your data be transferred to another entity.

To exercise these rights, contact us at itkonnects@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided such data, please contact us immediately at itkonnects@gmail.com, and we will promptly remove the information.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated clearly via the app or other communication channels. Please review this policy periodically.
